Frequently Asked Questions

Q: What audio formats are supported?

A: Bass & Vocal Booster supports MP3, WAV, OGG, and M4A formats. We can process virtually any audio format that your browser supports.

Q: Is my audio file stored on your servers?

A: No. All audio processing happens locally on your device. We never upload, store, or access your audio files.

Q: What web browsers are supported?

A: Audio Master works on modern browsers including Chrome, Firefox, Safari, and Edge. We recommend using the latest version for best performance.

Q: Can I use Bass & Vocal Booster on mobile?

A: Bass & Vocal Booster is optimized for desktop browsers. Mobile support is limited due to browser capabilities, but you can try it on your tablet.

Q: What's the maximum file size I can upload?

A: The file size depends on your browser and available RAM. Most modern browsers can handle files up to 1GB or more.

Q: Does Bass & Vocal Booster cost anything?

A: Bass & Vocal Booster is completely free to use. No registration or payment required.

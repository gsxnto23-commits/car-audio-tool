FAQs

Q: What audio formats are supported?

A: We support MP3, WAV, OGG, and M4A audio formats.

Q: Can I save my audio processing settings?

A: Currently, the tool processes audio on-the-fly. You can download the processed audio as a WAV file.

Q: Is my audio file stored on your servers?

A: No, all processing happens locally on your device. We don't store any audio files on our servers.

Q: Can I use this tool on mobile?

A: The Car Audio Tool is optimized for desktop and tablet devices. Mobile support is limited due to browser capabilities.

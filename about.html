🎵 About Audio Master

Our Mission

Audio Master is dedicated to providing professional-grade audio processing tools that are accessible to everyone. We believe that high-quality audio editing should not be complicated or expensive.

Features

  • Vocal Enhancement: Precisely adjust vocal frequencies to enhance or reduce vocals in your tracks
  • Bass Control: Shape the low-end frequencies to achieve the perfect bass level
  • Real-time Visualization: See your audio waveform in real-time as you make adjustments
  • Multiple Formats: Import and export in various audio formats including MP3, WAV, and OGG
  • Quality Selection: Choose from multiple export quality levels
  • Browser-based: No installation needed - works directly in your web browser

Technology

Audio Master uses the Web Audio API, a modern browser technology that allows for professional audio processing directly in your browser. All audio processing happens locally on your device - we never store or transmit your audio files.

Privacy & Security

Your privacy is our priority. All audio files are processed and stored only on your device. We don't upload, store, or analyze any audio content on our servers.

Getting Started

  1. Upload an audio file in any supported format
  2. Adjust the vocal and bass sliders to your preference
  3. Listen to your changes with the play/pause controls
  4. Select your desired export format and quality
  5. Download your processed audio
